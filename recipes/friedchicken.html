Fried Chicken

Description

Here's a recipe for an easy to make and delicious fried chicken!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds bonless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon gound black pepper
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • Cooking spray

Steps

  1. Place chicken thighs in a shallow dish. Pour buttermilk over chicken and refigerate for at least 4 hours.
  2. Preheat air fryer to 380 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  3. Mix flour, salt, and pepper in a large gallon-sized resealable plasic bag. Dredge chicken thighs in seasoned flour. Dip back into buttermilk, then coat with panko bread crumbs.
  4. Put chicken in air fryer and spray with cooking oil.
  5. Cook for 15 minutes. Flip the thighs and cook again for 10 minutes.
