Lasagna

Description

Here's a recipe for a delicious and easy to make lasagna!

Ingredients

  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomatoe sauce
  • Basil
  • Pasta Sheets
  • Shredded mozzarella
  • Ricotta
  • Ground Beef
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Half of a white onion

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients
  2. Cook ground beef, onion, and garlic in a dutch oven over medium heat until brown.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes and tomato sauce. While stirring add in basil.
  4. Cook pasta sheets in a large pot of boiling water for 8-10 minutes. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water when done
  5. In a mixing bowl combine ricotta and shredded mozzarella. Stir together.
  6. Prehead oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  7. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 6 pasta sheets over meat sauce. Put ricotta and mozzarella mixture on top of the pasta sheets. Repeat process until all pasta sheets have been used. Sprinkle the top with more mozzarella.
  8. Cover baking dish with foil and bake in the oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  9. Rest lasagna for 10 minutes before serving.
